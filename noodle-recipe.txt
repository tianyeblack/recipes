Ingredients:
 - 2 slice of bacon
 - 1 handful of noodle
 - 1 piece of sasuage
 - Half a box of tofu
 - Some random vegetable

Directions:
 1. Boil the water.
 
 2. Throw in tofu, sasuage, bacon.
 
 3. Wait until it boils again. Put noodle in.

 4. Wait until the noodle is soft enough to eat. Put in the vegetable.
